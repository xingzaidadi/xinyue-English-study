# Daily Review Rules

## Required Log Fields

Record:

- date
- planned tasks
- completed tasks
- time spent
- score or output
- error causes
- child mood
- parent notes
- tomorrow adjustment

## Error Categories

Use:

- vocabulary
- grammar
- task misunderstanding
- locating information
- inference
- listening sound/linking
- numbers/time/names
- writing content
- writing structure
- speaking fluency
- speaking expansion
- emotion
- unknown

## Adjustment Signals

Reduce difficulty if:

- frustration or resistance appears for two consecutive study days
- task completion is below half
- the child avoids speaking or writing repeatedly

Increase challenge if:

- accuracy is stable
- mood is calm
- tasks finish with time left

Keep challenge stable if:

- accuracy improves but the child still needs many prompts
- emotional load is normal but not low
