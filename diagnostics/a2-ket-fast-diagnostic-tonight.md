Date: 2026-06-08 Monday evening

Mode: Plan A

Goal: complete A2 Key for Schools Reading & Writing plus Listening tonight, then use the results to decide whether A2/KET is the right first target or whether selected B1/PET tasks should be tried next.

Open these links on the computer used tonight.

## 2. Tonight's Task

Estimated time:

- Reading & Writing: 60 minutes.
- Short break: 5-10 minutes.
- Listening: about 30 minutes.
- Parent recording: 5 minutes.

Total: about 95-105 minutes including break and recording.

If the child becomes tired or resistant, stop after Reading & Writing and do Listening another day.

## 3. Before Starting

Parent setup:

- [ ] Open the official A2 Key for Schools preparation page.
- [ ] Open or download the Reading & Writing sample test.
- [ ] Open or download the Listening sample test and audio.
- [ ] Open or download the answer keys if available.
- [ ] Open `diagnostics/results/2026-06-initial-diagnostic.md` for recording results.
- [ ] Prepare a timer.

Say this to Xinyue:

```text
Tonight we are not testing whether you are good or bad at English.
We are checking whether A2/KET is too easy, right, or too hard.
Please just do it normally. If you do not know something, make your best guess and continue.
```

## 4. Reading & Writing

Execution:

1. Start the timer.
2. Let Xinyue complete the A2 Reading & Writing sample test independently.
3. Do not translate words or explain questions during the attempt.
4. If she is stuck, say: "Make your best guess and continue."
5. Stop at 60 minutes.
6. Mark answers after completion.

Record:


## 5. Break

Take 5-10 minutes.

Do not review errors during the break.

## 6. Listening

Execution:

1. Let Xinyue read the questions before each part.
2. Play the audio according to the sample test.
3. Do not pause the audio during the first attempt unless the official task itself pauses.
4. Do not replay extra times during the first attempt.
5. Mark answers after completion.

Record:

```text
Listening:
Material used:
Parts completed:
Correct / total:
Accuracy:
Extra replay during first attempt:
Main missed information:
Mood:
Visible difficulty:
```

## 7. Do Not Do Tonight

Avoid:

- Detailed correction of every error.
- Vocabulary drilling after the test.
- A full speaking test after a long reading/listening session.
- Comparing the child with other children.
- Deciding on PET/B1 before seeing the scores and mood.

## 9. First Decision Signals

Use these only as rough signals.

| Evidence | Likely next step |
|---|---|
| Reading & Writing and Listening are both difficult | Do not test B1 yet. Use A2/KET route or foundation review. |
| Listening is strong but Reading & Writing is mixed | A2/KET first is likely safer; focus on reading/writing. |
| Reading & Writing and Listening are both manageable | A2/KET route is suitable; add writing/speaking output check next. |
| Reading & Writing and Listening are both easy, with calm mood | Try selected B1/PET tasks next before choosing route. |

Final route should be decided after reviewing scores, task completion, and emotional load together.
